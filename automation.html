SPOTIFY 'RECENTLY LIKED' PLAYLIST

I tend to just 'like' songs on Spotify, so they are added to my 'Liked Songs' playlist. However, as like most people, I want to listen to my most recently liked songs without listening to them in the same order every time, nor do I want to shuffle my entire 'Liked Songs' playlist. This led me to developing a quick script which updates a smaller 'Recently Liked' playlist with my 30 most recently liked songs.
