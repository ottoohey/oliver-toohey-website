/WORK-EXPERIENCE

I was fortunate enough to become a Grad at John Holland at the start of 2021 for my first full time job. After rotating through two teams, I got to work with a bunch of cool technologies, most of which I had no idea existed throughout university. Whilst neither team practiced what I predominantly studied at uni, being Software Development, I learnt so much about other parts of the IT industry which has added to my skillset enormously.


CLOUD INFRASTRUCTURE

Ansible has fast become the favourite part of my job, allowing me to approach all sorts of automation tasks with my Software Development background, whilst still exposing me to technologies I never got to work with at university. Most tasks included AD management using PowerShell, or Azure configuration using the Microsoft Graph API and really pushed me to learn to think about coding in a different way then I usually worked.



SYSTEMS & ENDPOINT

Most of my day to day was mainly sysadmin work, where we were responsible for user management, all devices, mailboxes, automation and our Azure systems.



BUSINESS INTELLIGENCE

My first rotation was in the Business Intelligence team, where I predominantly worked in MS SQL Server and Power BI to cover all the data and reporting for the company. I relied heavily on my interpersonal skills, as we often worked with users who knew what they wanted, but didn't necessarily have the technical background to implement so working together was crucial.
