/APP-DEVELOPMENT

Most of my app development has been done using Flutter. I really enjoyed how quick it was possible to develop with for any device. So far, I have only released one app, Project Cut, however I have worked on others which I have not been able to release yet for various reasons.


PROJECT CUT

At the beginning of 2021, I started working out after a lazy hiatus. It quickly became one of my favourite hobbies and I embraced the bulk/cut cycle. Upon getting sick of writing down on paper my progress, I decided to develop an app that not only let me track my progress, but provide calculated targets.





CLUB

During my time at university, I was a committee member of the Arts Society and as a result was heavily involved in organising events. I saw an opportunity to develop an app that acted as a hub for marketing, ticket sales and membership management for clubs on campus. Unfortunately, I never fully released it, but it exposed me to some of the complexities of developing an app with so many features.
